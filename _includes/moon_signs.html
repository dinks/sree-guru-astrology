What is a Moon Sign?

Moon Sign is purely based on one's birth star. It is an Indian system. Below are the signs.

  1. Mesha - Aswini, Bharani, 1/4 Krithika
  2. Vrishabha - 3/4 Krithika, Rohini, 1/2 Mrigasira
  3. Mithuna - 1/2 Mrigasira, Ardra, 3/4 Punarvasu
  4. Karkata - 1/4 Punarvasu, Pushya, Aslesha
  5. Simha - Magha, Poorvaphalguni, 1/4 Utharaphaguni
  6. Kanya - 3/4 Utharaphaguni, Hastha, 1/2 Chithra
  7. Thula - 1/2 Chithra, Swathi, 3/4 Visakha
  8. Vrischika - 1/4 Visakha, Anuradha, Jyeshta
  9. Dhanu - Moola, Poorvashadha, 1/4 Uttarashada
  10. Makara - 3/4 Uttarashada, Sravana, 1/2 Dhanishta
  11. Kumbha - 1/2 Dhanishta, Sathabhishak, 3/4 Poorvabhadrapada
  12. Meena - 1/4 Poorvabhadra, Utharabhadra, Revathi
