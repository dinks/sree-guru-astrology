What is a Sun Sign?

Sun Sign is purely based on one's date of birth only. It is a Western System. Below are the signs.

  1. Aries - (March 21 - April 20)
  2. Taurus - (April 21 - May 20)
  3. Gemini - (May 21 - June 21)
  4. Cancer - (June 22 - July 22)
  5. Leo - (July 23 - August 23)
  6. Virgo - (August 24 - September 22)
  7. Libra - (September 23 - October 22)
  8. Scorpio - (October 23 - November 22)
  9. Sagittarius - (November 23 - December 21)
  10. Capricorn - (December 22 - January 20)
  11. Aquarius - (January 21 - February 19)
  12. Pisces - (February 20 - March 20)
